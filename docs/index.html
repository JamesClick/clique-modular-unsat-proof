Abstract

This work introduces a modular computational signature for identifying UNSAT Boolean formulas by analyzing weighted sums modulo a prime number.

Conclusion

The absence of residues across orthogonal weights is a reliable indicator of formula unsatisfiability. The method shows promise across structured and random instances.
